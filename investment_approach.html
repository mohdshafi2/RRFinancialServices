Step One: Your Strategic Plan

If you already have an investment strategy in mind, StockCross.com offers the tools and resources you need to start investing.

  • View our online tutorials
  • Customize your dashboard: Real-time quotes, stock screeners, market news, and comprehensive third-party research.
  • Start trading: Quality executions and competitive commission rates.

If you need expert advice from our Investment Specialists, we will answer your questions and explain your options without cost or obligation.

  • We do not operate on quotas – there is no incentive to sell you one product over another.

StockCross SELECT Investment Advisor Representatives will handle all of your Wealth Management needs, so that you can spend your time enjoying life to the fullest.

Step Two: Manage Your Wealth

The StockCross website is engineered to help you expand your knowledge of the market. Dashboard tools you do not need today might come in very handy tomorrow. We are also continually introducing new resources to anticipate your evolving needs and changes in the marketplace.

Whether you are continually online, or only checking-in on occasion, real-time market data, portfolio performance and investment opportunities are always at your fingertips.

You do not need a computer to be a valued StockCross client and to take advantage of our services! We are happy to assist you in any way that is comfortable to you.

Step Three: It’s Your Money, Access It When You Need It

When your kids are ready for college, when you are ready for retirement, or when you simply need to have cash in hand, our Investment Specialists will help you liquidate and transfer your assets to your maximum financial advantage.
